About Us

Started in the year of 1997, Prince Products is  an ISO certified manufacturing and trading Company with a broad Range of Liquid Cleaner, Fabric Whitener, Perfumed Hand Wash, Dish Wash ,Cleaning Powders and much more.

In their development process, we assure that only top notch basic material is used along with ultra-modern tools and machinery. Besides this, we check these on a variety of grounds before finally giving them at the destination of our customers.
